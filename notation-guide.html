Chess moves are written using algebraic notation, which tells you:

  • Which piece moved
  • Where it moved to
  • (Sometimes) extra symbols describing captures, checks, promotions, etc.

Let's break it down.

  • Ranks (rows) labeled 1‚Äì8 from White's side to Black's side.

So each square has a coordinate like:

a1, e4, h7, etc.

üìå 2. What the Letters Mean

Pieces are abbreviated with capital letters:

Piece Letter
King K
Queen Q
Rook R
Bishop B
Knight N
Pawn no letter (just the square)

üìå 6. Castling
